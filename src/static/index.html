Why I Built This

Personal Journey

From Inspiration to Action

Greg Isenberg has accomplished so much and has so much incredible content scattered across different platforms and websites - all of it absolutely wonderful and valuable. But there was no single location where everything could be found together.

Greg's no-gatekeeping philosophy has helped thousands of people become millionaires. He's been extremely generous with his resources, sharing AI-oriented methods and amazing opportunities that have literally helped entrepreneurs build million-dollar platforms.

He's influenced over 1 million people through his content and communities, but Greg is so humble as an individual that maybe he would never build such a comprehensive, all-in-one "Gregverse" himself.

"I felt like somebody has to do it as a thank you to him."

Greg's videos inspired me to take action and build my own platform called PromptLink - a platform focused on collaboration between artificial intelligence agents, including orchestration and autonomous sessions between multiple agents designed to brainstorm, solve problems, and create technical solutions.

This comprehensive Greg Isenberg archive is my way of saying thank you to him, and hopefully it will be an asset to help him continue building his massive social media empire and influence.
